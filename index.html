Yusung Chu

I am a medical AI researcher developing clinically meaningful, robust, and interpretable models for medical image analysis, with a particular focus on dermatology and skin lesion imaging.

My research focuses on diagnostic support systems, multimodal medical image analysis, explainable AI, and generative models for improving data diversity and model robustness in clinical settings.

Medical AI Skin Lesion Imaging Deep Learning Explainable AI Generative Models

Biography

I received my B.S. and Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering from Yonsei University. Since September 2024, I have been working as a postdoctoral researcher in Precision Medicine at Yonsei University Wonju College of Medicine.

My work aims to bridge medical imaging, machine learning, and clinical decision support, especially for dermatology applications such as melanoma prediction, skin lesion analysis, and trustworthy AI systems.

Research Interests

  • Deep learning-based diagnostic support systems for dermatology
  • Multimodal medical image analysis using clinical and dermoscopic images
  • Robust and fair medical AI across diverse data conditions
  • Explainable AI and clinically interpretable visual concepts
  • Generative models for medical image synthesis and augmentation
